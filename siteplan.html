Bill Wallace & American Kickboxing

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Bill Wallace and the American Kickboxing

This name represents a website organization focused on promoting Bill "Superfoot" Wallace and Traditional American kickboxing to the world.

Domain availability: Kickboxing-Official.org or SuperfootKickboxing.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote Bill Wallace and American Kickboxing to the world by providing:

  • Comprehensive biography of Bill "Superfoot" Wallace
  • History of American kickboxing
  • Training techniques and exercises
  • Event calendars and tournament information

User Scenarios

  1. What is the history of American kickboxing and Bill Wallace's role in its development?
  2. Where can I find contact information for Bill Wallace's training seminars?
  3. What made Bill Wallace's "Superfoot" style unique?
